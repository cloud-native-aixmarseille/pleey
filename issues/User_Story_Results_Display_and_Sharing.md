### User Story
As a player and admin, I want to see the results of a question answering in a visually appealing and shareable format, so that the experience is enjoyable and engaging.

### Acceptance Criteria
1. Results are displayed in a visually appealing format.
2. Results can be shared through social media or other platforms.
3. The feature is accessible to both players and admins.

### Additional Notes
- Consider animations or charts for visual appeal.
- Ensure the sharing functionality complies with platform guidelines.
